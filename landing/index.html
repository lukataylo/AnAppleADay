The problem

The earliest signals go unmeasured.

People can't remember how they felt last week, so change creeps in unnoticed. The signals that drift first, a voice, a tired face, go untracked between appointments.

How it works

Ask. Listen. Hand off.

01 · ASK

One easy question

A single tap, with a face that reacts to your voice.

02 · LISTEN

On-device signals

Voice and face become numbers on your phone. The recording is never kept.

03 · HAND OFF

Ready for your GP

Trends against your baseline, and a summary you choose to share.
